Service Standards & FAQ

Everything you need to know about our executive cross-border transfers and premium chauffeur protocols.

Airport & Flight Protocols

How does the Meet & Greet service work at Vienna (VIE) & Bratislava (BTS)?

For a flawless arrival, your professional chauffeur will monitor your flight in real-time. They will enter the arrivals terminal exactly 15 minutes after you land, holding a professional digital tablet presenting your name or your company logo. They will assist you fully with your luggage and escort you directly to your prepared premium vehicle.

What happens if my flight into Vienna or Bratislava is delayed?

There are absolutely no surcharges for delayed flights. Because we require your flight number during booking, our dispatch network tracks your aircraft live. Your pickup time is automatically adjusted to your actual landing time, guaranteeing your chauffeur is standing ready when you exit customs.

How much complimentary waiting time is included?

All airport pickups include up to 60 minutes of complimentary waiting time from the moment your aircraft wheels touch the tarmac. This provides ample, stress-free time to pass through border control and retrieve your luggage. Standard address pickups include 15 minutes of complimentary waiting time.

Vehicles & Executive Standards

What conditions and standards can I expect from the fleet?

Luxovia operates an immaculate, strictly black Mercedes-Benz fleet (including S-Class luxury sedans, E-Class executive sedans, and V-Class premium vans). Every vehicle is deeply detailed, completely sanitized, and presented to corporate road standards before your journey. Complimentary chilled bottled water and high-speed Wi-Fi are provided on all cross-border routes.

Are your chauffeurs fully licensed and English-speaking?

Yes, absolutely. Every single Luxovia driver is a professional commercial chauffeur holding a valid Slovak transport license (*Koncesia*). They are completely bilingual, highly experienced with diplomatic and private aviation protocols, and have extensive geographic knowledge of the Vienna, Bratislava, and Budapest corridors.

Payments & Corporate Corporate Accounts

What payment methods do you accept?

We support secure payment workflows tailored for corporate travelers. You can pay seamlessly online during booking, request a secure card payment link via Revolut, or pay your chauffeur inside the vehicle via credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). For corporate accounts, we offer monthly consolidated invoicing.

How can we set up a Corporate Account for our enterprise?

Embassies, luxury business hotels, and corporations can open a dedicated client profile. This grants priority fleet availability, access to custom cross-border flat-rates, dedicated account logs, and streamlined billing options. Please reach out via our Contact page or email us at booking@luxovia.eu to establish terms.
