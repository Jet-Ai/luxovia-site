Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before booking a Luxovia chauffeur transfer.

Do you provide Bratislava airport transfers?

Yes. Luxovia provides premium Bratislava airport transfer service to hotels, offices, private addresses and long-distance destinations.

Can I book a Vienna airport transfer from Bratislava?

Yes. Bratislava to Vienna International Airport and Vienna Airport to Bratislava are among our most requested routes.

Do chauffeurs speak English?

Yes. Luxovia is multilingual-ready and prioritizes English-speaking chauffeurs for international travelers and corporate clients.

Can I confirm by WhatsApp?

Yes. Booking forms prepare a WhatsApp confirmation message so our team can respond quickly with availability and pricing.

Do you support corporate accounts?

Yes. Corporate accounts can include recurring transfers, priority communication, ride notes and monthly invoicing.

Is Luxovia a taxi service?

No. Luxovia is a premium chauffeur and executive transport service focused on scheduled rides, discretion and high service standards.
